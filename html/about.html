About Us

Welcome to the unofficial Love, Lois website

Love, Lois aspires to make you feel as beautiful as you actually are through jewelry. Not only that, every piece is handcrafted with care and love. This shop believes that you deserve to wear special jewelry and not mass-produced ones.

How The Business Started:

Before she turned eighteen, Stefanie Lois Garcia recognized she needed a source of income. She knew she'd be receiving money from her godparents and elders, and she didn't want that money to be wasted. She then spent weeks looking for small business ideas before coming across something about handcrafted polymer clay earrings. This drew her interest since she was impressed by the ingenuity and unlimited possibilities of polymer clay jewelry manufacturing. She had 3,000 pesos in her hand as her birthday came to a close. This was adequate to purchase the necessary materials. She began with only two clay colors: white and black. She also made the earrings by hand, without the necessary tools or equipment to make her job easier. As she invests in increasingly expensive materials, tools, and equipment, she is gradually able to expand her firm. She also continued to acquire new ways to ensure that her products met or exceeded her consumers' expectations.
