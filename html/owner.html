About the Owner

Stefanie Lois Garcia is the sole owner of Love, Lois at the age of 19 years old. She was born on August 20, 2002 and currently residing in the City of Malabon where she takes up the course Bachelor of Arts in Communication currently in University of the East Caloocan. Lois also studied in Jose Rizal Campus, Arellano University and now in University of the East Caloocan till this day. In high school, Lois graduated in Arellano University and Jose Rizal Campus. Stefanie Lois Garcia realized she needed a job before she turned eighteen. She knew she'd be receiving money from her godparents and elders, and she didn't want that money to be wasted. She then spent weeks looking for small business ideas before coming across something about handcrafted polymer clay earrings. This drew her interest since she was impressed by the ingenuity and unlimited possibilities of polymer clay jewelry manufacturing. She began with only two clay colors: white and black. She also made the earrings by hand, without the necessary tools and equipment to make her job easier. As she invests in increasingly expensive materials, tools, and equipment, she is gradually able to expand her firm. She also continued to acquire new ways to ensure that her products met or exceeded her consumers' expectations.
